# Legal Disclaimer

Lawz AI JO Ultra-MVP is an educational capstone MVP only. It is not a legal product and does not provide legal advice.

The system:

- Does not decide whether a clause is legal or illegal.
- Does not recommend signing or not signing a contract.
- Does not draft lawsuits.
- Does not advise whether a user can sue.
- Only flags predefined potential risks for review.

Any output should be treated as a first-pass checklist and reviewed with a qualified lawyer in Jordan.
